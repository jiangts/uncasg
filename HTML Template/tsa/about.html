About TSA

The Technology Student Association is a non-profit national organization which devotes itself to providing technology education to young people interested in STEM-related fields.

Students in TSA have the opportunity to compete at the local, state, and national level in competitions which range from Architectural Renovation to Webmaster.

Our TSA Chapter

Our Chapter is a relatively new chapter (only 3 years old), yet we have recieved multiple trophies at the state level, and are competing at the National level this year.
